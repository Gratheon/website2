# Legal Documents

This page contains all legal documents related to contributing to Gratheon OÜ projects and commercial licensing.

## For Contributors

### Contributor License Agreement (CLA)

Before contributing to Gratheon projects, you'll need to sign our Contributor License Agreement. This allows us to maintain dual licensing (open source AGPL v3 and commercial licenses).

- **[Individual CLA](./CLA-INDIVIDUAL)** - For individual contributors
- **[Corporate CLA](./CLA-CORPORATE)** - For companies whose employees contribute

**How to sign:**
1. When you submit your first pull request, our CLA Assistant bot will ask you to sign
2. Click the link and sign digitally (takes ~2 minutes)
3. Alternatively, download the PDF, sign, and email to pilot@gratheon.com

## Licensing Model

Gratheon OÜ uses a **dual-licensing model**:

### Open Source (AGPL v3)
- Free to use
- Must share modifications if hosting publicly
- Source code available
- Community support

### Commercial License
- Proprietary use allowed
- No source disclosure required
- On-premise deployment
- Priority support & SLA
- Custom integrations

See our [Pricing Page](https://gratheon.com/pricing) for details.
