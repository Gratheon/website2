## Overview

i-bee is a European web-based beekeeping management platform with multi-language support, targeting professional beekeepers across Europe.

## Product Features
- Web-based hive management
- Multi-language support (European markets)
- Digital record keeping
- Hive inspection logging
- Queen and colony tracking
- Treatment management
- Harvest planning
- Multi-apiary support
- Professional features

## Competitive Analysis

### Strengths
- Multi-language European platform
- Professional beekeeper focus
- Established presence in Europe
- Comprehensive management features
- Web-based (no installation required)
- Multi-apiary capabilities
- European beekeeping practices knowledge
- Cross-platform accessibility

### Weaknesses vs Gratheon
- No IoT hardware integration
- No computer vision or AI
- No automated monitoring
- Manual data entry required
- Pure software solution
- No real-time sensor data
- No predictive analytics
- Limited automation

### Strategic Implications
- European web app competitor (same geography)
- Professional market overlap
- Multi-language support is competitive necessity
- Shows demand for comprehensive management tools
- Gratheon's hardware integration is key differentiator
- Potential migration target for automation upgrade
- Learn from their multi-language implementation

## Market Position
- Established European web app
- Professional beekeeper segment
- Multi-country presence

## Competitive Strategy
- Position Gratheon as "automated i-bee"
- Offer data migration/import from i-bee

## Lessons for Gratheon

## Differentiation for Gratheon
