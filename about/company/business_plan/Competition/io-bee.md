## Overview

io-bee is a European IoT sensor platform for beehive monitoring with focus on the European market and professional beekeepers.

## Product Features
- IoT sensor monitoring system
- Hive weight tracking
- Temperature and humidity sensors
- European market focus
- Professional-grade hardware
- Data analytics platform
- Mobile and web applications
- Real-time alerts

## Competitive Analysis

### Strengths
- European domain and market (same as Gratheon)
- Professional beekeeper target
- EU compliance (GDPR, standards)
- Local European support
- Multi-apiary management
- Real-time monitoring
- European beekeeping standards knowledge
- Established in European market

### Weaknesses vs Gratheon
- No computer vision capabilities
- No entrance monitoring
- No frame-level inspection
- Basic sensors only (weight, temp, humidity)
- No AI/ML predictive features
- No automated image analysis
- Limited innovation beyond standard sensors

### Strategic Implications
- **Direct European competitor** in same market
- Same target customer segment (semi-pro)
- Same geographic focus
- Monitor their feature additions closely
- Price competitively
- Differentiate with computer vision
- European market requires strong differentiation

## Market Position
