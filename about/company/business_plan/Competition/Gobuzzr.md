## Overview

Gobuzzr is an IoT sensor platform for beehive monitoring targeting hobbyist and semi-professional beekeepers with affordable monitoring solutions.

## Product Features (Typical)
- Hive weight monitoring
- Temperature sensors
- Humidity tracking
- Mobile app access
- Data visualization
- Alert notifications
- Cloud-based platform
- DIY-friendly installation

## Competitive Analysis

### Strengths
- Affordable pricing for hobbyists
- Entry-level market (large TAM)
- Easy installation and setup
- Mobile app accessibility
- Real-time monitoring
- Alert system for anomalies
- Cloud platform (accessible anywhere)
- DIY approach reduces costs

### Weaknesses vs Gratheon
- No computer vision capabilities
- No entrance monitoring
- No frame-level inspection
- Basic sensor features only
- No AI/ML predictive analytics
- US market focus (not European)
- Limited innovation
- Manual interpretation required

### Strategic Implications
- Entry-level competitor (different price tier)
- Geographic separation (US vs Europe)
- Shows affordable sensor market exists
- Hobbyist segment has different needs than semi-pro
- Gratheon can offer "upgrade path"
- Basic monitoring is table stakes
- Need differentiation through automation

## Market Position
- Entry-level IoT sensors
- Hobbyist primary target
- US market focus
- Affordable pricing positioning

## Competitive Strategy
- Position Gratheon as "next level" beyond basic sensors
- Target semi-pros who outgrow basic monitoring
- Emphasize computer vision advantage
- Highlight automation vs manual interpretation
- Price competitively in semi-pro segment

- Easy setup reduces barriers

1. Computer vision (visual monitoring)
